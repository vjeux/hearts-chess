♥ 3-Hearts Chess

Every piece has 3 ♥. Attacked pieces lose a heart (0 = gone). Your KING loses a heart on each check — 3 checks and you lose!

♥ How to Play — 3-Hearts Chess

Standard chess, but every piece has hearts and can survive being attacked — until it can't.

♥ Hearts

Every piece starts with 3 hearts (shown above it). Hearts let a piece take a few hits before dying.

💥 The damage phase

At the end of every move, damage is applied only to your opponent's pieces — the side that just moved never takes damage on its own turn.

  • A piece loses one heart for every enemy piece attacking it. Attacked by two pieces → lose two hearts.
  • A piece at 0 hearts is removed from the board.
  • Blocking/defending doesn't stop heart loss — if it's attacked, it loses hearts.

♟ Capturing

You can still capture normally — move onto an enemy square to take the piece outright, no matter how many hearts it had.

♚ The king

The king also has 3 hearts. You still can't move into check, and checkmate still ends the game. Because you never leave your own king in check, it only takes damage from the opponent's check — so it loses exactly one heart per check.

🏆 How to win — two ways

  1. Checkmate the enemy king, or
  2. Check the enemy king three times — each check drains a heart; at 0 hearts, that player loses.
